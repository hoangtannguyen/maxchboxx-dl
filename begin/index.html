OUR STORY

Matchbox is a family owned Australian kitchenware, giftware and homewares retailer.

We stock popular brands such as Avanti, Scanpan, Jamie Oliver by Tefal, Yumbox, Sachi, Pyrolux, Essteele, Woll, Kilner, Cuisinart, Global, Krosno, Thermos, Maxwell & Williams, Contigo, Keepcup, Chasseur and Zyliss.

Founded in Victoria, Australia in 1975, the first store opened on High St, Armadale. We now have over 20 stores across Victoria, Queensland and Western Australia.
